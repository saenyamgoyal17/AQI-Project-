About Project

Intelligence

Advanced atmospheric monitoring system developed as part of the MDP Project. Focused on providing high-precision air quality metrics using localized environmental sensor data.

Education

Built by first-year undergraduate students pursuing BTech at VIT Vellore.

Goal

To build a comprehensive safety advisory network that empowers citizens with actionable data to combat urban pollution and improve overall respiratory health outcomes.

Live Telemetry

Real-Time Sync

Real-time tracking of PM2.5 particulates, atmospheric density, and environmental health status across global urban centers using cloud-synchronized data streams.

Advisory

Health Advisory

Real-time guidance generated from AQI levels using EPA standards. The system helps users decide when outdoor activity is safe, when to reduce exposure, and when to stay indoors.

Sensitive Group Alerts

Additional precautions are recommended for children, elderly, and people with respiratory conditions when AQI rises above moderate levels. The advisory engine highlights these risks clearly.
